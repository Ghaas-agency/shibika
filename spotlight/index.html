What is Shibika Spotlight?

A Shibika Spotlight gets you a centre-stage with the right audience for your art. A Spotlight is a personalised exclusive page, designed, created and hosted on Shibika that shares your journey and experience as an artist with the world.

In a single digital space, we map your work, your persona and let people know you beyond your art. Using that, you not only get an online profile for yourself, you also reach a wider audience and increase your fanbase.

How to get a Shibika Spotlight?

Wish to get a Shibika Spotlight for yourself too?
It's three simple steps away:

Sign up on Shibika.
Complete your Shibika profile by adding your detailed bio, works, offerings, events, etc.
Answer a quick questionnaire to help us know you better and share relevant resources.

That’s all we’d need from you to build your Spotlight.

Be rest assured, we’d keep you in the loop at each step as we progress towards preparing your digital footprint.
Content and designs would be executed only post your approval.
